49  1) compute the centroid (c0, c1, c2) and the normalized covariance
50  2) compute the eigenvectors e0, e1, e2. The reference system will be (e0, e1, e0 X e1) --- note: e0 X e1 = +/- e2
51  3) move the points in that RF --- note: the transformation given by the rotation matrix (e0, e1, e0 X e1) & (c0, c1, c2) must be inverted
52  4) compute the max, the min and the center of the diagonal
53  5) given a box centered at the origin with size (max_pt.x - min_pt.x, max_pt.y - min_pt.y, max_pt.z - min_pt.z) the transformation you have to apply is Rotation = (e0, e1, e0 X e1) & Translation = Rotation * center_diag + (c0, c1, c2)
54 
55  cheers
56  --N
57  */
